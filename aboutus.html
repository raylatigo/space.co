Our Mission

Our Brownsville Coworking Space is perfect for individuals who wish to work from a professional office space, and for companies that want an upgraded work space. At SPACE, we pride ourselves on the atmosphere we've cultivated to empower professionals in our community. We can't wait to meet you and be a part of your growth.

What Makes Us Different?

Our Space

Housed in a repurposed lumber yard, people frequently comment on the warm aesthetics and coffee-house vibe of our interior space. The exterior is equally unique, as it features a large lighthouse or beacon, which we see as a symbol of the involvement we look forward to having in the community.

Our People

SPACE is devoted to building community both among members and within the surrounding neighborhood. By hosting numerous events, such as concert series, book clubs, and movie screenings, we facilitate connections and conversations between individuals representing a wide variety of backgrounds, interests, and vocations.

Our Impact

You may have noticed our name features this work, ‘impact’. To us, having an impact means implementing positive, sustainable, and measurable change into the community and beyond.
